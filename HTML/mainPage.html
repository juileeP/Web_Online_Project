What We Do

was created to deliver food to hungry students from the campus favourites that don't deliver themselves. Our goals are efficiency and Customer-service. We want your experience to be quick and easy from start to finish. Let us know, if it's not. We love your feedback.

How We Do It

Sign in with your name and cell number on our order page. Choose some awesome food just how you like it. We'll pick up your food and bring it to the main door of your dorm or apartment within forty-five minutes. It's that easy.

Who We Are

Rebbeca Leisher opened Beck and Call Food Delivery in the Fall semester of 2013. She formed the website with much help from Juilee Patankar and Summer Schneider and currently co-ordinates all activities involving deliveries, promotion, correspondence with participating restaurants and public relations.

Rebecca plans to continue to expand her service to include more restaurants, more dormitories, and eventually other universities. Rebecca is currently pursuing her Bachelor of Science degree in Economics with Minors in Business Administration and English at the University of Delaware. She also served as Treasurer of University of Delaware chapter of Project Sunshine and is a member of Women in Business.
